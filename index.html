About

I am a Red Seal Endorsed (RSE) electrician and will be completing my fourth period instrumentation and control technician at NAIT in April 2025. During my electrical apprenticeship I worked in residential, commercial, and industrial construction.

I have E&I experience with installation, troubleshooting, and commissioning. I have been involved with two Atco hydrogen projects including FSOC and Home One.

As an instrumention tech, I have used Swagelok fittings and tubing. I have calibrated chart recorders, EFMs, transmitters, and switches.
